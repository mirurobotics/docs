Provisioning a device is the process of creating it in Miru and installing the Miru Agent on it. Once provisioned, a device is ready to receive deployments.

The Miru dashboard is the simplest way to provision devices, ideal for getting started or managing individual devices.

For automated deployments or provisioning devices at scale, consider using [API Keys](/docs/learn/devices/provision/api-keys) instead.

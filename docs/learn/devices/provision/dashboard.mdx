Provisioning a device is the process of installing the `miru-agent` package and activating the device in your workspace. Once provisioned, a device is ready to receive deployments.

The Miru dashboard is the simplest method, ideal for getting started or managing individual devices.

For automated deployments or provisioning devices at scale, consider using [API Keys](/docs/learn/devices/provision/api-keys) instead.

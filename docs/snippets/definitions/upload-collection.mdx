An **upload collection** is a named grouping of [upload rules](/data-uploads/upload-rules/overview) — the analog of a [config type](/cfg-mgmt/config-types) for data uploads. Every upload rule belongs to exactly one collection, and rules are deduplicated by digest within it. Collections give the data your fleet records a stable, human-readable identity (`Robot Logs`, `Crash Reports`) that survives across the immutable rules that come and go beneath it.
